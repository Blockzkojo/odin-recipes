Sloppy Joes Recipe

A delicious and appetizing Sloppy Joe

It is best served with the combination of Browned ground beef, green bell peppers, and onions in hearty tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Brown Sugar
  • Hot Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Green Bell

    • Steps for making Sloppy Joes

    1. Cook the beef, onion, and green pepper
    2. Drain the liquids, then stir in the remaining ingredients.
    3. Simmer for about 30minutes
    4. Serve on hamburger buns
